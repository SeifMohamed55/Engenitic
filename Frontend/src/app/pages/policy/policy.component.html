# Privacy Policy for Engenetics **Effective Date: June 13, 2025** Engenetics ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, Engenetics, a free English learning platform. By accessing or using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. ## 1. Information We Collect We collect the following types of information: ### 1.1 Personal Information - **Account Information**: When you register as a student, instructor, or unverified instructor, we collect your name, email address, username, password, and, for instructor applicants, a CV. - **Profile Information**: Any updates to your username, email address, or password provided through your profile page. - **Course Interaction Data**: Information about your enrolled courses, completed quizzes, progress tracking, and course reviews. - **Authentication Data**: If you use Google authentication, we collect your Google account email and associated profile information to facilitate login. ### 1.2 Non-Personal Information - **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the website, such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and search queries in the courses tab. - **Device and Technical Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers. - **Cookies and Session Data**: We use cookies to manage user sessions, track preferences, and improve user experience. ### 1.3 Uploaded Content - **Instructor CVs and Images**: CVs and images uploaded by users applying to become instructors are stored via Cloudinary. - **Course Content**: Videos and other materials uploaded by instructors, hosted on third-party cloud services (e.g., Twitch, Dropbox, Google Drive). ## 2. How We Use Your Information We use the collected information for the following purposes: - **Account Management**: To create and manage user accounts, verify instructor applications, and facilitate password resets via email. - **Service Delivery**: To provide access to courses, track progress, deliver quiz results, and display personalized course recommendations. - **Communication**: To send email notifications for password resets, email change confirmations, and other account-related activities via Gmail API. - **Website Functionality**: To enable features such as course searching, pagination, and responsive design. - **Security**: To authenticate users via JSON Web Tokens (JWT) and refresh tokens, manage sessions with cookies, and protect against unauthorized access. - **Analytics and Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and improve the website’s functionality and user experience. ## 3. How We Share Your Information We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following cases: - **Third-Party Service Providers**: With trusted providers like Cloudinary (for CV and image storage), Gmail API (for email communications), and Google Cloud Console (for authentication and integration). - **Legal Compliance**: When required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Engenetics, our users, or the public. - **Instructor Verification**: Admins may access CVs and account details to review and approve instructor applications. ## 4. Data Storage and Security - **Storage**: Personal information is stored in a PostgreSQL 17 database using a code-first approach with Entity Framework Core. CVs and images are stored via Cloudinary, and course videos are hosted on third-party cloud services. - **Security Measures**: We use JWT and refresh tokens to secure API endpoints, ASP.NET Identity for user management, and environment variables to protect sensitive data. The frontend is secured with Angular guards, and the backend follows onion architecture for modularity. - **Cookies**: We use cookies to manage user sessions and ensure seamless navigation. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. ## 5. Your Rights and Choices - **Access and Update**: You can view and edit your username, email, or password in your profile. Email changes require confirmation via a message sent to the new email address. - **Account Deletion**: Contact us to request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal obligations. - **Cookies**: You may disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect website functionality. ## 6. Third-Party Services Engenetics integrates with the following third-party services: - **Google Authentication**: For student login, subject to Google’s Privacy Policy. - **Gmail API**: For sending password reset and email confirmation messages. - **Cloudinary**: For storing CVs and images uploaded by users. - **Cloud Services (Twitch, Dropbox, Google Drive)**: For hosting course videos, subject to their respective privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies. ## 7. Data Retention We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. Account data is retained until you request deletion, and course progress data is stored to maintain your learning history. CVs and uploaded content may be retained for instructor verification purposes. ## 8. Children’s Privacy Engenetics is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, we will take steps to delete it. ## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. ## 10.
